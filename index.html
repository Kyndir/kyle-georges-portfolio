Case Studies

A closer look at how the work got done.

01

Content Systems & Automation

Replacing manual busywork with a repeatable content system.

Problem

Content scheduling, file organization, and reporting were handled manually across scattered folders and spreadsheets, consuming hours every week that could go toward creative work.

Goal

Design a repeatable system that automates the administrative side of content production without limiting creative flexibility.

My Role

Founder & Creative Director - owned strategy, workflow design, and implementation end to end.

Technologies

MetricoolCanvaClaude API

Process

Audited the existing manual posting and captioning workflow, then paired Metricool’s scheduling and publishing tools with Claude API-assisted caption drafting to keep a 90+ post/month calendar running across Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.

Challenges

This was run independently, with the client’s own operations partners handling day-to-day administrative coordination - keeping caption quality consistent across a high volume of AI-assisted drafts, without it reading as generic, was the main challenge.

Solution

Used Claude API to draft and refine captions against a consistent brand voice, then scheduled and published everything through Metricool - cutting per-post production time by an estimated 60% without adding headcount.

Measurable Outcomes

0Estimated reduction in per-post production time using Claude API-assisted captioning
0Posts per month scheduled across Instagram, Facebook & TikTok
0Additional headcount added to run the expanded content operation

Lessons Learned

AI-assisted drafting only saves time when paired with a real review step - keeping a human pass in the loop kept captions from reading as generic.

02

Video Production Pipeline

A standardized shoot-to-edit workflow for consistent, professional social video.

Problem

Video quality and turnaround time varied from project to project, making it hard to plan client content calendars with confidence.

Goal

Create a dependable shoot-to-edit pipeline that delivers cinematic, on-brand video within tight social media turnaround windows.

My Role

Founder & Creative Director - shooter, editor, and process owner.

Technologies

Sony A7RIIDJI Ronin-SC2CapCut Pro

Process

Shot every project on a Sony A7RII paired with a DJI Ronin-SC2 gimbal for stabilized, cinematic movement, then edited and exported everything in CapCut Pro using platform-specific presets for each format.

Challenges

Tight deadlines and multiple format requirements - stories, reels, and ads - meant footage had to be reusable across very different aspect ratios and lengths.

Solution

Shot with multi-format reuse in mind from the start, then used CapCut Pro’s export presets to move from a single edit straight to platform-ready versions for stories, reels, and ads.

Measurable Outcomes

0Increase in Airbnb search-to-listing click-through rate (18.91% → 33.55%), driven in part by professional photography & listing optimization
0Post FIFA World Cup 2026 campaign produced end-to-end across Meta Ads & organic social
0Branded property guidebook designed and produced for client listings

Lessons Learned

Planning for reuse during the shoot saves far more time in editing than trying to fix format problems after the fact.

03

AI-Assisted Content Workflows

Using AI as an amplifier for creative work, not a replacement for it.

Problem

Ideation and first-draft writing for client content calendars took up creative hours that could be spent refining ideas instead of generating them from scratch.

Goal

Integrate AI tools responsibly into the content and voice workflow to speed up drafting while keeping every brand's voice authentic and consistent.

My Role

Founder & Creative Director - designed the workflow and prompt approach, reviewed and edited every output.

Technologies

Claude API (Anthropic)ChatGPTElevenLabsMetricoolCapCut Pro

Process

Built brand voice guidelines for each client, used Claude API and ChatGPT for first-draft copy and ideation, ElevenLabs for AI-assisted voiceover on video content, then scheduled everything through Metricool with a human review step before anything shipped.

Challenges

Generic AI output was the biggest risk - without careful prompting, drafts and voiceovers felt the same across different brands.

Solution

Paired detailed brand-voice prompts with a consistent editing checklist across every AI tool in the stack, so AI-assisted drafts and voiceovers still sounded distinctly on-brand before publishing.

Measurable Outcomes

0Reduction in client average booking lead time (27.7 → 14.5 days) during this workflow period
0Performance analysis deck with a full SWOT analysis & 90-day growth roadmap, drafted with AI assistance
0Return on ad spend (ROAS) on a $48 Meta Ads launch-day campaign from the same engagement

Lessons Learned

AI speeds up the blank page problem, but brand voice and final judgment still have to come from a human who knows the client.
